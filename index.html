Architecture

How Neural Networks Learn

Stacked layers of neurons transform raw inputs into high-level abstractions through millions of learnable parameters.

  • Input layer receives raw features (pixels, words, numbers)
  • Hidden layers extract increasingly abstract representations
  • Activation functions introduce nonlinearity for complex mappings
  • Backpropagation computes gradients and updates weights iteratively
